# Shipping Information

## Standard Shipping

Estimated delivery:
3–5 business days.

## Express Shipping

Estimated delivery:
1–2 business days.

## Free Shipping

Orders above $75 qualify for free standard shipping.

## Order Processing

Orders are typically processed within one business day.

Orders placed during weekends or public holidays are processed on the next business day.

## Tracking

A shipment tracking number is emailed once the order has been dispatched.

## International Shipping

Currently we only ship within the United States.
