# Frequently Asked Questions

## What products do you sell?

Northwind Outfitters sells casual clothing including t-shirts, hoodies, jeans and jackets for men and women.

## How do I know if an item is available?

You can ask our shopping assistant whether a specific product or SKU is currently in stock. Inventory is checked in real time across all warehouses.

Example:
- Do you have Blue Classic Cotton T-Shirt in Medium?
- Is SKU NP-TSH-BLU-M available?

## How do I know the price?

Our pricing service always returns the latest active price.

Prices may change during promotions and seasonal sales.

## Can I order multiple quantities?

Yes. You can purchase multiple units of the same product if inventory is available.

## Which sizes are available?

Most apparel is available in:

- Small
- Medium
- Large
- Extra Large

Availability depends on the specific product.

## Which colors are available?

Color availability varies by product.

Examples include:

- Black
- Blue
- White
- Red
- Green

## Do you have physical stores?

Yes. We operate retail stores in multiple cities across the United States.

You can ask:
"Where is your Chicago store?"

## Can I return products?

Yes. Eligible products can be returned within 30 days of delivery if they are unused and in their original packaging.
